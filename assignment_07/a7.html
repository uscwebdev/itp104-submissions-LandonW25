CTF Project Proposal

Description

This website will be a collection of my experiences with CTF Hacking competitions, with demos, expereiences, and relevant further reading all available. It will also serve as a portfolio for my offensive cybersecurity career

Audience

This is intended for anyone interested in laerning or practicing CTfs, as well as potential recruiters and CTF makers.

Design

I intend to incorporate monochromatic colors(Primary black and white), with a green seconday color scheme that is similar to the text in The Matrix, as a nod to its cultural relevance to hacking and early internet era websites. Inpiration: Matrix Movie Website / Visual Insipration CTF Website / Content Inspiration USC Web Dev / Structural Inspiration

Scope

I plan on having a page for my experiences/examples of CTFs, a page within that for problem explanations ,a page for further learning about CTFs, a page for my personal portfolio/contact, and a page for getting involved with USC's CTF team. So 4-5 pages depending on how I choose to implement the problem solving aspect.
